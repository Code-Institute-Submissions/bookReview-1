What is Never Judge A Book?

Never Judge A Book is a website for people to come and view book reviews added by other users. It is a close and intimate site allowing users to add their favourite books and what they thought and their not so favourite books and what they thought!

If you are a book lover, this site is perfect for you. All you need to do is log in for the site and you can log in whenever suits you and simply uplaod your review.

We hope you like this website, we encourage all of our members to give a good detailed review of all the books they have read. There will also be extra features coming to the site soon, such as a blog and a book recommendation page. Enjoy your reading!
